# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.

Contributors must communicate constructively, critique ideas rather than people, respect privacy, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or disclosure of another person's private information. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method listed in the organization profile. Good-faith security research and technical disagreement are welcome when handled responsibly.
